Robert Frost

Robert Lee Frost, born March 26, 1874 in San Francisco, Calfiornia to William and Isabelle. After his father's death from tuberculosis when he was 11, Robert along with his sister and mother moved to Lawrence Massachuseets Robert became interested in reading and writing poetry during high school. He later enrolled at Darmouth College in 1892 and later at Harvard University, although he never earned a degree.
