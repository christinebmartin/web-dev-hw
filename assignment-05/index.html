Smoked Pork Belly Burn Ends

Ingredients

  • 4-5 lbs pork belly
  • 2-3 Tbsp olive oil

Rub

  • 1/4 c cup brown sugar
  • 2 Tbsp coarse Kosher salt
  • 1 Tbsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Sauce

  • 1/2 to 1 cup BBQ Sauce
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/3 cup honey

Directions

  1. Trim off the skin and top layer of fat

    2. You can ask the butcher to trim the skin for you, but you run the risk of them taking off quite a lot of the top layer of fat. We do like to trim off the fat on top when it is pretty thick, but some people love the fat and like to leave most of it on. A lot of it will render off anyway. You donâ€™t want to trim it all away, but our preference is to trim some, if not most, of the areas of pure fat away. To do this, just use a very sharp knife to make gashes horizontally across the top of the piece of pork belly, peeling away the top layer of skin and fat as you go.

  2. Cut meat into cubes
